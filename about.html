About Us

On December 1, 2015, MC Online Radio was created with the intention to provide a custom built radio application for the Western Massachusetts area. With the power of UberStations API , MC Online Radio has been able to stream most of the local radio stations to its visitors. After the completion of the website, a visitor suggested adding a universal call letters player. The addition of this player has allowed an extensive new network of radio stations able to be accessed.
